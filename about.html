My Story

I grew up being curious about how technology works, especially the devices and systems people use every day. From a young age, I was interested in computers, gadgets, and problem-solving, even if I did not fully understand how everything worked at first. Over time, that curiosity grew into a deeper interest in learning how hardware and software come together to create useful solutions.

I chose to pursue Computer Engineering because it combines two areas that I find exciting: technology and innovation. I like the idea of not only using computers but also understanding how they are designed, programmed, and improved. Computer Engineering gives me the opportunity to explore programming, electronics, systems design, and problem-solving in a practical way. It challenges me to think logically, be creative, and continuously learn new things.

My journey has not always been easy, but every challenge has helped me become more determined. There are times when lessons, projects, and technical topics can feel difficult, but I see them as part of the process of growth. Each experience teaches me patience, discipline, and the importance of staying focused on my goals.

Outside of academics, I am interested in technology trends, digital tools, and innovations that can make life easier for people. I enjoy learning skills that can help me improve not only as a student but also as a future professional. I believe that technology has the power to solve real-world problems, and I want to be part of that progress.

My goal is to become a skilled and responsible Computer Engineer who can contribute meaningful ideas and solutions. I want to keep developing my knowledge, gain hands-on experience, and prepare myself for opportunities in the technology field. What drives me is the desire to improve, to build something useful, and to create a future where my skills can make a positive impact.
